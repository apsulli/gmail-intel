Gmail Intel Mail Tracker Extension

What does this app do?

Gmail Intel Mail Tracker Extension is a browser extension designed to help you track when the emails you send are opened. It seamlessly integrates into your existing webmail interface to provide read receipts and link click tracking.

Key Features:

  • Track email opens
  • Track link clicks within your emails
  • View analytics on recipient engagement
