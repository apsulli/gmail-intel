Privacy Policy for Gmail Intel Mail Tracker Extension

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Gmail Intel Mail Tracker Extension collects data essential for tracking email opens and link clicks, including:

  • Email metadata (Subject, Time Sent)
  • Open and click events (Timestamps, IP addresses, User Agent)
  • Your Google account identifier to associate your tracked emails

2. How We Use Information

We use this information exclusively to provide the email tracking dashboard within your Gmail interface. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Gmail API Usage

Gmail Intel Mail Tracker Extension's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We use the Gmail API solely to enable the sending of tracked emails on your behalf when you actively use the "Track" feature.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely using Firebase. It is protected by security rules ensuring only you can access your own tracking data.
